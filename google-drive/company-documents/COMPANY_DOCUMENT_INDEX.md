## Setup Instructions

### Using Macro Sheets
1. Download CSV from Google Drive
2. Import to Google Sheets
3. Go to **Extensions > Apps Script**
4. Paste corresponding `.gs` file contents
5. Save and refresh sheet
6. Look for custom menu in menu bar
